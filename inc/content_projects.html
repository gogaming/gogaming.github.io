Touch Portal

Disclaimer: I am not the developer of Touch Portal and I am not associated with it in any way.

This is a piece of amazing software and I really like a lot and use it on a daily basis. I'll not copy and paste the full details of what it does here but I will say it allows you to control a PC or Mac remotely using devices like mobile phones and tablets. Don't take my word for it, just head on over to Touch Portal download and install it for FREE. If you do use this software I highly recommend you join the Touch Portal discord server. I am just a regular user that has tested some devices I own with it, and other users have told me that works too, so decided to compile a list of devices below.

If you have any devices with other operating systems or devices that work with Touch Portal, please drop me an email on the Contact page or contact Starzze on the Touch Portal Discord server.
